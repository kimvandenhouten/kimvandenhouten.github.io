# Kim van den Houten

# About
KIM VAN DEN HOUTEN is a PhD candidate at Delft University of Technology. She holds a Masters Degree in Operations
Research (VU Amsterdam). Her research interests are on the intersection of optimization, machine learning, and simulation. Her email address
is K.C.vandenhouten@tudelft.nl.

# AI4b.io
I will obtain my PhD at the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory for Bioscience (AI4b.io). This lab is a collaboration between DSM and Delft University of Technology. AI4b.io is aiming at long-term innovation in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Bioscience to develop biobased products and to optimize biobased production technologies. In my PhD, I look at scheduling algorithms for large-scale biomanufacturing facilities.
